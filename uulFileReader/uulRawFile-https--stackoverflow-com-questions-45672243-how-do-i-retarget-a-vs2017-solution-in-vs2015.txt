<li>Right click on the project in the Solution Explorer</li>

<li>Navigate to "<strong>Configuration properties</strong>"</li>
<li>Click on the "<strong>General</strong>" option below "Configuration properties"</li>
<li>Change "<strong>Platform toolset</strong>" to <em>Visual Studio 2015 (v140)</em></li>
